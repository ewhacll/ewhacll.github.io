detail: "This study aims to design optimal prompt engineering based on individual profiling in order to maximize the effects of intervention for children’s social communication problem-solving, and to develop a validated evidence-based intervention manual following intervention using generative AI. The study will further examine the generalization effects of the developed manual and explore the sustainability of integrated everyday-life treatment outcomes. Ultimately, it seeks to establish an academic foundation necessary to support successful social communication."
