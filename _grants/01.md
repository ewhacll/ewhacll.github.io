detail: "This study aims to develop XR-based therapeutic content for addressing social communication disorders by utilizing familiar character IP embedded in existing completed content, and to investigate its effectiveness. This content-based approach is meaningful in that it can be integratively linked to situation understanding, emotion inference, and social problem-solving processes. In addition, by providing a context similar to real-life communication situations, it has clinical value in enhancing contextual understanding and applicability in social problem situations."
